How I approach it

I don't try to reinvent the wheel. I look at what's already working, then replicate it and improve it a bit. Sometimes that means combining a couple of ideas into something new.

A lot of it is just paying attention. Figuring out why a video works, then applying that to whatever I'm marketing, and testing a lot until something lands.

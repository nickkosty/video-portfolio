How I approach it

I don't try to reinvent the wheel. I look at what's already working in the niche, and in nearby niches, then replicate the formats that are doing well and improve on them a bit. Sometimes that means combining a couple of ideas into something new.

A lot of it is just paying attention. Figuring out why a video works, then applying that to whatever I'm marketing. I'm willing to test a lot until something lands.

What I can do
  • Run organic content day to day, posting consistently to find what works
  • Send a short weekly update on what I tested, the numbers, and what I'm trying next
  • Start small and prove it out before anything bigger
